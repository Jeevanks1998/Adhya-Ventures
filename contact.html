Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response after submitting the form?

Our team responds to all enquiries within 24 working hours. For urgent queries, you can also reach us directly via phone or WhatsApp for an immediate response.

Do you offer a free initial consultation?

Yes! We offer a complimentary 30-minute consultation to understand your project requirements, discuss timelines, and give you a high-level cost estimate — completely free of charge.

Which areas in Bengaluru do you serve?

We serve all major areas across Bengaluru including Whitefield, Electronic City, Jayanagar, Koramangala, Hebbal, Nagadevanahalli, and surrounding regions. We also take up projects outside Bengaluru — please contact us to discuss.

What types of projects do you handle?

We handle end-to-end residential and commercial construction, architectural design, interior design, and renovation projects. From single homes to large commercial complexes — we do it all.

Can I visit your office before starting a project?

Absolutely. We welcome you to our office at KCHS Layout, Nagadevanahalli, Bengaluru. You can walk in Monday through Saturday between 9 AM and 6 PM, or book an appointment in advance.

How is your pricing structured?

Our pricing is transparent and project-specific. After the initial consultation, we provide a detailed quotation based on area, materials, scope, and timeline. We also offer flexible package plans — check our Packages section for details.
